💡 Travel Tips

🔋

Universal Adapters

A good universal adapter covers most countries. Look for one that handles Types A, B, C, G, and I for maximum coverage.

⚡

Voltage Matters

Most modern electronics (laptops, phones) are dual-voltage (100-240V). Check the label on your charger before traveling.

🔄

Converters vs Adapters

Adapters change the plug shape. Converters change the voltage. You may need both for older devices.

🏨

Hotel Tip

Many international hotels provide adapters at the front desk or have universal sockets in rooms. Ask when you check in!
